## üí∞ Sales Metrics

### Total Revenue
**Description**  
Total monetary value generated by completed orders.

**Formula**  
`SUM(price + freight_value)`

**Grain**  
Order item

**Usage**  
- Global performance tracking
- Category and regional analysis
- Trend analysis over time

---

### Total Orders
**Description**  
Number of unique customer orders.

**Formula**  
`DISTINCTCOUNT(order_id)`

**Grain**  
Order

**Usage**  
- Volume tracking
- Demand analysis
- Funnel indicators

---

### Average Order Value (AOV)
**Description**  
Average revenue generated per order.

**Formula**  
`Total Revenue / Total Orders`

**Grain**  
Order

**Usage**  
- Commercial efficiency
- Pricing and bundling analysis

---

## üöö Delivery Metrics

### Delivered Orders
**Description**  
Number of orders successfully delivered to customers.

**Formula**  
`COUNT(order_id)` where `order_status = 'delivered'`

**Grain**  
Order

**Usage**  
- Logistics throughput
- Operational performance

---

### On-Time Delivery Rate
**Description**  
Percentage of delivered orders that met the promised delivery SLA.

**Formula**  
`Delivered on-time orders / Delivered orders`

**Grain**  
Order

**Usage**  
- SLA monitoring
- Logistics reliability

---

### Average Delivery Days
**Description**  
Average number of days between purchase and delivery.

**Formula**  
`AVG(delivery_days)`

**Grain**  
Order

**Usage**  
- Customer experience monitoring
- SLA optimization

---

### SLA Distribution
**Description**  
Distribution of delivered orders across SLA buckets.

**Buckets**
- ‚â§ 7 days (Fast)
- 8‚Äì15 days (Standard)
- 16‚Äì30 days (Slow)
- > 30 days (Late)

**Grain**  
Order

**Usage**  
- Bottleneck identification
- Service-level diagnostics

---

### Cumulative Delivery Performance
**Description**  
Cumulative percentage of orders delivered within X days.

**Formula**  
Cumulative sum of delivered orders ordered by delivery_days.

**Grain**  
Order

**Usage**  
- SLA tipping point analysis
- Delivery promise optimization
